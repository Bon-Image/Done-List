Home Concept

The “done list,” as shared by Oliver Burkeman, offers a refreshing alternative to the usual to-do list. Instead of obsessing over what’s next, we pause to acknowledge the things we’ve already achieved—no matter how small. This simple practice can help quiet the constant pressure of productivity, offering a sense of accomplishment and presence.
